The Story of our Coffee

A Unique Blend of Community and Quality

This cozy rooftop cafe offers breathtaking city views, creating a peaceful haven above the urban chaos. The name itself, "Roof Cafe," reflects their aim to be a refuge where you can relax and forget your everyday troubles.

The beans are sourced directly from local farmers, which ensures quality and fair methods. We carefully choose beans to bring out the unique features of Philippine coffee. Whether you choose traditional or innovative brewing methods, each cup is a work of art that exemplifies The Roof Cafe PH's dedication to excellence and heritage.

Our Beans

The Roof Cafe PH's dedication to excellence begins with the meticulous selection of coffee beans supplied directly from local growers throughout the Philippines. We believe in promoting local talent and highlighting the vast diversity of Philippine coffee culture.

Roasting

Each batch of beans goes through a tough method in which research and skills combine to achieve their full potential. Every step, from carefully selecting beans to accurately setting the temperature and timing, was carried out with continuous attention to detail. As the beans evolve in the roaster, bringing out a symphony of smells, our commitment to creating the perfect roast is unmistakable. It's a journey of discovery and mastery, with each crackle and burst signaling the birth of a rich, aromatic treasure ready to be appreciated in every cup.
